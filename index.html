Common Questions
Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before enrolling — coaching and hostel both.

Does ALLOY Academy provide Class XI & XII HS Board classes alongside coaching? +
Yes, ALLOY Academy is a junior college cum coaching institute. Students receive regular AHSEC Board classes for Class XI and XII alongside their NEET or JEE coaching. You do not need to attend a separate school — both are covered here under one roof.
Is the hostel on the same campus as the academy? +
Yes, both boys and girls hostels are on the same campus. They are completely separate buildings with their own warden staff. Facilities include 3 meals daily, Wi-Fi during study hours, a dedicated study room and library, laundry service, CCTV surveillance and 24/7 warden supervision.
What room types are available in the hostel? +
We offer sharing rooms (2–3 students) and single occupancy rooms. Sharing rooms are the most popular and available for both boys and girls. Single rooms are limited. Contact us for current availability and pricing for both options.
Is there a scholarship or fee concession available? +
Yes! Students scoring 90% or above in Class X board exams are eligible for a merit scholarship on coaching fees. Students from economically weaker backgrounds may apply for a fee concession. Coaching fees can be paid in installments. Contact us for full details.
When do new batches start for 2025–26? +
New batches begin in June 2025 for XI Science and the 2-year NEET/JEE programs. The dropper batch starts in August 2025 after NEET results. Hostel allotment happens alongside enrollment. Early registration is strongly recommended as seats are limited.
Can parents visit hostel students? +
Yes, parents are welcome on designated visiting days with prior notice to the warden. The academy provides monthly performance reports and is reachable by phone for any concerns about hostel students at any time.
Is there a dedicated dropper batch for students retrying NEET or JEE? +
Absolutely. Our Dropper Batch is intensive, focused on targeted weak-area improvement, revision and mock testing. Many of our best results come from dropper students. Hostel is fully available for dropper batch students too.
How are mock tests and assessments conducted? +
Chapter-wise tests every week, unit tests monthly, and full-length mock exams every two months. All NEET mocks follow NTA pattern and JEE mocks match the actual format. Results and analysis are shared with students and parents each month.
